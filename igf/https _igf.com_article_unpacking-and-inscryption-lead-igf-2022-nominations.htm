  <h2>'Unpacking' and 'Inscryption' Lead in IGF 2022 Nominations</h2>
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p>The nominees for the 2022 <a href="https://igf.com/" target="_blank"><strong>Independent Games Festival Awards</strong></a> have arrived. Whether you're risking it all in an inky black card-based odyssey, or simply organizing your new one-bedroom apartment—this year in indie games is something special.</p>
<p>Organizers of the <a href="https://gdconf.com/passes-prices/?_mc=blog_x_gdcsfr_un_x_gdcsf_x_x-10" target="_blank"><strong>2022 Game Developers Conference</strong></a> are thrilled to unveil this year's finalists for the 24th annual IGF Awards—part of the longest-running festival, summit, and showcase celebrating independent games and their creators. <a href="https://igf.com/inscryption"><em>Inscryption</em></a> by Daniel Mullins Games and <em><a href="https://igf.com/unpacking">Unpacking</a> </em>by Witch Beam lead the pack with four nominations each: Excellence in Audio, Excellence in Design, Excellence in Narrative, and the Seumas McNally Grand Prize.</p>
<p><em>Inscryption</em> is a genre-bending love letter to video games that blends the deckbuilding roguelike, escape room-style puzzles and psychological horror into a spooky package. Quite differently, <em>Unpacking</em> is a zen game about the familiar experience of pulling possessions out of boxes and fitting them into a new home, learning clues about the person's life along the way.</p>
<p>The Independent Games Festival is part of GDC 2022, which will be held <strong>in-person in San Francisco and virtually from March 21-25</strong>.</p>
<p> Here are the other nominees for the Seumas McNally Grand Prize:</p>
<p>●      <a href="https://igf.com/cruelty-squad"><em>Cruelty Squad</em></a> (Consumer Softproducts): A tactical first-person shooter where players are an emotionally dead combat-substance fueled grunt of Cruelty Squad, a depraved subsidiary company tasked with performing wetworks for its host conglomerate (also nominated for the Nuovo Award).</p>
<p>●      <a href="https://igf.com/loop-hero"><em>Loop Hero</em></a> (Four Quarters): Players help the brave hero to defeat the Lich that has thrown the world into a timeless loop by wielding an expanding deck of mystical cards, which place enemies and terrain to help challenge the hero and, in doing so, strengthen them.</p>
<p>●      <em><a href="https://igf.com/eternal-cylinder-0">The Eternal Cylinder</a> (</em>ACE Team): Players control a herd of adorable creatures as they explore a strange alien world filled with exotic lifeforms and surreal environments—all while under the constant threat of the Cylinder, a gargantuan rolling structure of ancient origin that crushes everything in its path (also nominated for Excellence in Visual Art).</p>
<p>●      <em><a href="https://igf.com/unsighted">Unsighted</a></em> (Studio Pixel Punk): A sci-fi adventure where players' choices have powerful consequences. As the Automaton Alma, players traverse a vast world searching for hidden passages, powerful weapons, and critical tools—while facing dire decisions in a race against time to save their friends from a tragic fate.</p>
<p>This year's nominees were selected from a list of over <a href="https://igf.com/entries/2022/" target="_blank"><strong>400 entries</strong></a>, thanks to the hard work of our expert juries in each individual category (more on them <a href="https://igf.com/article/here-are-igf-juries-nuovo-design-and-narrative-awards-gdc-2022" target="_blank"><strong>here</strong></a> and <a href="https://igf.com/article/these-are-igf-juries-audio-visual-art-and-student-game-awards-gdc-2022" target="_blank"><strong>here</strong></a>)—who chose the finalists after playing, discussing, and meticulously evaluating each of the games in consideration.</p>
