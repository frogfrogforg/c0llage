you think about how gross flowers are.

delicate, intricate things

their patterns fractalling inwards

or at least that's what you remember

from where you're standing, you can't really make out the finer details

You hear feet stamping on leaves and turn to see someone approaching.

"Hey, sorry."

Their head is shaved, and you notice the tattered loops of a backpack over a dark crop top. Their eyes are locked onto you.

"Have you seen anyone walk by? Ginger? A little taller than me? General air of idiocy?"

"Nevermind, of course you haven't."

They sit down on a nearby log and start rummaging through their pack.
